Introducing SWACC: A Framework for Sustainable Income in Extractive Industries

By Khaled Aldhaferi

Khaled Aldhaferi is a finance scholar and practitioner with over 20 years of experience in the oil and gas industry. He holds a BSc in Accounting and Finance from CSU (Colorado, USA), an MSc in Accounting and Finance from Cardiff Metropolitan University (UK), and an MBA in Finance from INSEEC Paris with Financial Manager certification. Khaled is also a part-time lecturer in accounting and finance.
